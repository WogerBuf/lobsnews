Look on the Bright Side

Good things are happening all the time, but bad news travels faster. Come on in and read about some of the positivity out there today.

The good that's easy to miss, from anywhere in the world — the environment and the creatures in it, people being good to each other, science and health that helps, hard-won human feats, real progress on fairness, and the small player beating the giant. No politics. Two checks before anything appears: one finds it, a second re-verifies it against a reputable source and throws out whatever’s overblown or unproven. What’s left says what happened, what backs it, and, where we think it might be helpful, what it doesn’t mean.
